At **PackingWala.com**, we use a **3-Tier Architecture** to deliver a fast, secure, and scalable booking experience for packing & moving services.

## Architecture Overview

### 1. Presentation Layer
User-facing website and mobile interface that ensures smooth navigation and quick bookings.

### 2. Application Layer
Handles business logic such as booking workflows, pricing, vendor assignment, and notifications.

### 3. Database Layer
Securely stores customer data, orders, vendor details, and transaction records.
